About YoPro


YoPro T&T is the Trinidad and Tobago arm of YoPro Global; an organization dedicated to the advancement of youth and young professionals focused on professional development. Using the Experience Economy, YoPro is able to engage youth and young professionals and support their active participation in professional development and personal growth. Visit www.eypglobal.com to learn more about YoProT&T's and YoPro Global's mission to increase employability and decrease un and underemployment worldwide.
